John Forbes Nash Jr.

Legendary mathematician defined by a Beautiful Mind

Here's the timeline of John Forbes Nash's Jr life:

1928
Born in Bluefield, WV.
1930
Diagnosed with dedivression.
1948
Received his graduate and bachelor's degree from Carnegie Institute of Technology (now Carnegie Mellon University).
1950
Received his doctorate from Princeton University.
1957
Married Alicia Larde.
1959
Alicia became pregnant with their son.
1963
Nash marriage to Alicia ended in a divorce.
1978
He was awarded John von Neumann Theory Prize.
1994
Was awarded the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economic Science for his work on the mathematics of game theory.
1999
He was awarded the Leroy P Steele divrize.
2001
He married Alicia a second time.Nash biography was adapated as a movie A Beautiful Mind which won Academy Award.
2012
He was an inaugural fellow of American Mathematical Society.
2015
Nash died in a car crash in New Jersey, on his was back home with his wife after receiving the Abel Prize in Oslo a few days prior.
